who am i?

Creative generalist in Game Development, Marketing, Video Editing and Music Production

For all my life I’ve been creating. When I was 5 I wrote my first book. I made my first board game when I was just 8, started programming and music production at 13. All throughout this time I was trying anything and everything creative. Painting, film making, stop motion animation, marketing and game development.

I’ve completed a Bachelor of Software Engineering at Media Design School, majoring in Game Programming, and a Diploma in Creative Marketing at Yoobee. Now in my life, I’m eager to continue this journey working in the industry and further developing my skills.
