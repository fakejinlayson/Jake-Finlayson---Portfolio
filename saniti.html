SANITI

Marketing collateral for SANITI, the student association for Nelson Marlborough Institute of Technology. Here, I created social media graphics, and event posters for various campaigns.

Marketing

Half of my role was managing SANITI's marketing channels, which included creating social media graphics, event posters, updating their Wordpress website and other marketing collateral. It was important to maintain consistent design across all platforms, so each campaign had a unique theme but still felt like part of the same brand.

SANITI Birthday Reel

Apart from graphic design, I also created motion design content for social media. This included short videos and reels to promote events and campaigns.

For these, assets were created in Illustrator and Photoshop then bought across to Premiere Pro for basic animation.

Events

The other half of my role was organizing and photographing events. This included coordinating people, managing logistics, and ensuring everything ran smoothly on the day.
