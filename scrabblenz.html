Scrabble NZ

Livestream producer and coordinator for Scrabble NZ events. As well as, creating video essay style content for YouTube to promote NZ Scrabble.

About

Contracted as the NZ Scrabble Livestream Producer, showcasing top games (Masters, Nationals, etc) on YouTube. The role involves a multi-cam setup whilst streaming in commentators from across the globe to OBS and going live. Additionally, I had further contracts with Scrabble NZ making a YouTube documentary style video promoting Scrabble in New Zealand. This project involved using GeoLayers in Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro for editing, iZotope Rx11 to master audio and Photoshop for any graphics.
