Game Grimoire YT

A YouTube Channel I've started focused on breaking down the Game Design and Psychology behind games.

About

The channel is a personal project of mine where I explore various aspects of game design, development, and the psychological principles that make games engaging and enjoyable. To create these videos I use a combination of gameplay footage mixed with motion graphics created in Abobe Illustrator and After Effects. For the audio I record using Abobe Audition, mix and master using iZotope RX11 and then collate all clips in Premiere Pro.
