tides under all

About

Originally began as part of the OST Composing Jam of 2024, which I ended up scoring 39th out of 650 contestants. I further refined the concept and published it online to major DSP's such as Spotify with a new album cover I illustrated.

The album blended Botanica genres with Hyperpop elements whilst remaining stylistically a Videogame OST. Through the tracks the genre explores someones full life, from birth to death and what's after. Using symbology of the water, the album shows that the tides are apart of us all.

To achieve this sound, I used FL Studio, combining organic instruments, granular synthesis and digital synthesis.
